Pretzels and Skull—A Variable Typeface

Pretzels and Skull—A Variable Typeface

typeface and specimen book design
(thesis project)

Glyphs | Adobe InDesign | Adobe Photoshop | Adobe Illustrator

Digital representation plays a critical role in the popularity and usage of a language. A lack of this is threatening the Tamil language spreading among a new generation of users. I will be designing a Tamil and Latin digital variable typeface that will help in the increase the language's popularity and usage.

Pretzels and skulls—a multiscript typeface available in both Tamil and Latin script. The typeface consists of 500 (approx.) characters including alphabets, numerals, punctuation, and symbols. It has five weights; light, regular, medium, bold and extrabold it's also avaiable as a variable font. This typeface is designed for the purpose of combining print and digital publishing. Its large x-height gives the possibility of typesetting texts in smaller sizes. The precise letter production by including ink traps, large counterspace and apertures preserve good readability even on small screens. OpenType features enable typesetting in both languages.

The specimen is a 4-inch by 6-inch, 16-page book. The following are spreads from the specimen book.
